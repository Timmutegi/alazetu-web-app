Get To Know AlaZetu

AlaZetu is a Kenyan company that curates music education material. Our aim is to increase the amount of Kenyan material in the music education space.

Our Story

Our Founder's Perspective

Hello, and welcome! My name is Makau, founder of AlaZetu.

Our journey began back in 2020 when as a music teacher based in Nairobi, I faced a serious shortage of Kenyan music material to use with my students in class. How could I teach Kenyan musical styles without Kenyan material? From that time I did not rest until I had engaged a number of Kenyan musicians whose music was just right for use in teaching, rallied teachers like myself as well as music arrangers, and got down to the business of curating a series of music education books for as many instruments as we could. Say hello to the Ngomabooks!

What The Future Holds

Join us, be it by learning Kenyan sounds through the Ngomabooks, or by recommending them to family, friends and colleagues, as we work towards a future that is rich in Kenyan music education material. We look forward to a future where we can learn and enjoy our rich musical heritage, a future which, with you in it, will be all the more delightful.
